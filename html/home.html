About

PhantomFinder is an asymmetrical board game for two players: ghost and ghost hunters.

Setup

  • Create a game by selecting a side: ghost or ghost hunters.
  • Join a game with a game ID.
  • Every game is played on an 8x8 grid with random obstacles.

Gameplay

  • The ghost can move like a Chess Knight: 2 spaces vertically or horizontally, then 1 space perpendicular.
  • The hunters can move like Chess Queens: any number of spaces vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
  • If a hunter has a direct line-of-sight to the ghost, the ghost will be revealed.
  • If the ghost moves onto a space with a hunter, the hunter disappears, and the ghost moves again.
  • The hunters player can choose between moving an existing hunter or adding a new one at the edge of the board.

Endgame

  • If a hunter moves to the ghost's space, the hunters win.
  • If the ghost captures 4 hunters, the ghost wins.
